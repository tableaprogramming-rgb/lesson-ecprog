Key Concepts to Remember

  • Modulo Operator (%): Returns the remainder of division
  • Even Numbers: Have remainder 0 when divided by 2 (n % 2 == 0)
  • Odd Numbers: Have remainder 1 when divided by 2 (n % 2 == 1)
  • Conditional Inside Loop: Test condition on every iteration
  • Accumulation: Only increment when condition is TRUE
